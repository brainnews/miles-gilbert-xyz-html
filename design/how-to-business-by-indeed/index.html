How to Business by Indeed

Making business education accessible and engaging

Creative Director • Indeed • 2021

As creative director for Indeed's How to Business series, I led the development of a series of videos and visual assets that made business education accessible and engaging. The project included creating a series of short films that demonstrated key business concepts in a fun and approachable way. Through compelling storytelling and innovative visual design, we made business education more accessible to a broad audience.
