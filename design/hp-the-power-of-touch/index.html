HP The Power of Touch

Exploring the sensory experience of touch technology

Creative Director • HP • 2012

As creative director for HP's The Power of Touch campaign, I led the development of a series of videos and visual assets that explored the sensory experience of touch technology. The project included creating a series of short films that demonstrated the power of touch in everyday life, as well as a series of print ads that highlighted the tactile nature of HP's touch-enabled devices. Through compelling storytelling and innovative visual design, we made the case for why touch technology matters.
