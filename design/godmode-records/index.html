Godmode Records

Defining an independent music label's visual identity

Designer • Godmode Records • 2013

Overview

Godmode Records is an independent music label that has launched the careers of artists like Channel Tres and LoveLeo. As creative director, I developed the visual identity and creative assets that would help the label stand out in a crowded independent music landscape.

Outcome

The visual identity helped establish Godmode Records as a distinctive voice in independent music, supporting the label's ethos of experimentation and genre-bending music.
