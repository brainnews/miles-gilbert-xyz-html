IBM Smarter Planet Series

Exploring the future of technology and society

Creative Director • IBM • 2019

As creative director for IBM's Smarter Planet Series, I led the development of a series of videos and visual assets that explored how technology could make the world work better. The project included creating multiple video series, each focusing on different aspects of IBM's vision for a smarter planet. Through compelling storytelling and innovative visual design, we demonstrated how IBM's solutions were helping to solve complex global challenges.
