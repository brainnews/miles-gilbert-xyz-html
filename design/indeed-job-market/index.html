Indeed Job Market

Transforming job fairs into immersive career experiences

Creative Director • Indeed • 2021

As creative director for Indeed Job Market, I led the development of a series of immersive career events that transformed traditional job fairs into engaging experiences. The project included creating a series of videos and visual assets that showcased the unique atmosphere and impact of these events. Through innovative design and compelling storytelling, we created spaces where job seekers and employers could connect in meaningful ways.
