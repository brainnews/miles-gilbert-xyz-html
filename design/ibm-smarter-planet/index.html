IBM Smarter Planet

Visualizing a more intelligent world

Creative Director • IBM • 2010

As creative director for IBM's Smarter Planet campaign, I led the development of a series of visual assets that brought to life IBM's vision for a more intelligent, interconnected world. The project included creating a series of images that demonstrated how technology could make our world more efficient, sustainable, and connected. Through compelling visual design, we made the case for why a smarter planet matters.
