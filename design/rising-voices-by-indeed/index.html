Rising Voices by Indeed

Celebrating underrepresented filmmakers with a joyful visual identity

Creative Director • Indeed • 2025

Overview

Rising Voices is Indeed's annual indie film incubator dedicated to amplifying underrepresented voices. As Creative Director, I led the development of year 5's identity system.

The visual identity is a celebration of color and energy. It uses playful, trendy design language—bubbly typography, vibrant sunburst patterns, and a bold orange-and-cyan palette—to create an optimistic, youthful tone that deliberately centers the filmmakers, with Indeed stepping back to play a supporting role.
