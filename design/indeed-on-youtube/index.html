Indeed on YouTube

Creating engaging career content for YouTube

Creative Director • Indeed • 2020

As creative director for Indeed's YouTube channel, I led the development of a series of engaging video content that helped job seekers navigate their career journeys. The project included creating multiple video series, each with its own unique style and approach to career advice. Through innovative storytelling and visual design, we created content that resonated with millions of viewers and helped them find their next opportunity.
