Metitate.art

Metitate.art is a daily art discovery platform featuring curated works from The Metropolitan Museum of Art. Using a custom algorithm, it surfaces intriguing objects based on age, materials, and artist significance. Users can build personal collections, creating their own digital museums from the Met's vast archives.

Shooot

Shooot was an iPhone camera app that simulated film photography by hiding photos until the next day and encouraging viewfinder-free shooting. It aimed to remove digital distractions and embrace happy accidents.
