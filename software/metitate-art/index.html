Metitate.art

A portal to mindfulness, discovery, and the joy of reading art

Developer, Designer • Self-directed • 2022

Metitate.art is a daily art discovery app that curates works from The Metropolitan Museum of Art using a custom algorithm.

This algorithm prioritizes the most intriguing objects based on factors like age, materials, and artist renown.

Users can also collect objects by adding them to their personal collection. This feature creates a sense of ownership and a deeper connection with art, as each collected artifact becomes exclusive to the user.
