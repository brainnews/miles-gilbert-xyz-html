Metitate.art

A portal to mindfulness, discovery, and the joy of reading art

Developer, Designer Self-directed 2022

Metitate.art is my portal to mindfulness, discovery, and the joy of reading art. Over the past 2 years, it has evolved into a personal art history companion, guiding my personal exploration of the vast collection of The Metropolitan Museum of Art.

Each day on metitate.art, a new gallery is unveiled, showcasing objects ranging from European masterpieces to ancient sarcophagi. An algorithm of my own design prioritizes the most intriguing objects based on factors like age, materials, and artist renown.

Users can curate their own digital museums by collecting objects and adding them to their personal collections. This feature fosters a sense of ownership and encourages a deeper connection with art, as each collected artifact becomes exclusive to the user.
