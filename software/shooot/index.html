Shooot

The camera for happy accidents

Creator, Creative Director, Product Designer Self-directed 2016

Shooot was a camera app for iPhone that removed the distractions of smartphone photography. The app simulated the waiting period of old school film development by asking users to wait until the next morning to see their photos.
