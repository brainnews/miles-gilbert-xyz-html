Work Sounds by Indeed

Harmonizing work and sound

Creative Director, Designer Indeed 2023

Collaborating closely with my team and producers at You Had Me at Black, I played a key role in the development of Work Sounds, an original album designed to enhance the workday. I helped shape the album's narrative, which combines workplace recordings with melodic instrumentals.

Leading the visual component of the project, I conceptualized, produced, and edited videos for YouTube and TikTok, ensuring the album's unique concept was effectively communicated across platforms.
