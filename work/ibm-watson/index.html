IBM Watson

Bringing artificial intelligence to life

Creative Director IBM 2018

As creative director for IBM Watson, I led the development of a series of videos and visual assets that brought the world's most advanced AI system to life. The project included creating multiple video series that explored Watson's capabilities, from its historic Jeopardy! appearance to its real-world applications. Through innovative storytelling and visual design, we demonstrated how Watson was transforming industries and helping solve complex problems.
