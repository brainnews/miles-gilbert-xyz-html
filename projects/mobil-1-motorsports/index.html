Mobil 1 Motorsports

As art director for Mobil 1's motorsports campaign, I brought scientific sophistication to the world of high-performance racing. Leading both NASCAR and Formula 1 initiatives, I developed a visual narrative that transformed complex engineering into compelling storytelling. The campaign unified television and digital content around a central truth: in motorsports, milliseconds matter as much as molecules. Working in close partnership with copywriters and directors, I shaped a visual aesthetic that elevated Mobil 1 beyond traditional sponsorship, showcasing how advanced oil technology becomes pure racing performance.
